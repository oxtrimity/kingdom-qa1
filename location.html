Location


The compound is ideally situated for easy access from virtually every part of Riyadh. It is located at the junction of Takhagususi Street and King Abdul Aziz Road. It is a 20-minute drive from King Khalid International airport, 15 minutes from the diplomatic quarter and 10 minutes from all major shopping centers.
