Kingdom City

Residences

Kingdom City offers you a world of fine and quality housing. The beauty of Najdi architecture combined with all the comforts of a western lifestyle provides an amiable environment for a perfect stay. Consisting of 333 living accommodations with full support facilities, the City offers a range of housing facilities that present and are tailored to meet your needs. All villas feature centrally air-conditioned (roof mounted systems), American/European standards furniture, soft packs, PBX (direct telephone lines are available on demand), front gardens, private rear gardens, shaded individual car parking beside daily entrance of each villa, in addition to many other facilities
